# Privacy Policy for Chrome Resizer Extension

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

Thank you for using the Chrome Resizer Extension. Your privacy is critically important to us. We have designed this extension with the utmost respect for your personal information by ensuring everything operates securely on your local device.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, read, store, or transmit any of your personal data.** 
The Chrome Resizer Extension operates entirely locally. It does not monitor your browsing behavior, track your web history, and it does not have access to the content of the web pages you visit. 

## 2. Permissions Explained
The extension requests the following permissions strictly to provide its core functionality:
* **Storage (`storage`):** Used solely to save your custom window size presets and preferences locally within your browser. This data never leaves your device.
* **System Display (`system.display`):** Used securely and locally to determine your screen's physical dimensions and boundaries. This ensures the extension can accurately resize, scale, and position your browser window without exceeding your active monitor.

## 3. Data Sharing and Selling
Because we do not collect any user data, **we absolutely do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third parties whatsoever.** We fully comply with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

## 4. Remote Code and Tracking
This extension is self-contained. It does not load or execute any remote code, and it does not utilize any third-party analytics or tracking tools.

## 5. Transparency
The Chrome Resizer extension is an open-source project. We believe in complete transparency. You can inspect the source code to verify our privacy claims at our official GitHub Repository.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the permissions used by the extension, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
